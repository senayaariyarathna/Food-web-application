    Flour based food baked in an oven such as cakes,cookies,pastries and pies.Baked goods have been around for thousands of years. The art of baking was developed early during the Roman Empire. It was a highly famous art as Roman citizens loved baked goods.

    Archaeologists have dated grinding stones in Australia as being more than 30,000 years old. This makes Aboriginal people the world's first bakers.

    The Egyptians were also pioneers in baking as the first recorded civilization to use yeast in their bread as long ago as 2600 BC.
